House of Vera is an independent production company run by Anna Champion and Zinnia Vroland.
We produce/ direct/ create music videos/short films and anything in-between.

We would like to acknowledge that we are working and creating on the traditional lands of the

Boon Wurrung and Woi Wurrung (Wurundjeri) peoples of the Kulin Nation, and we pay our

respects to Elders both past and present.

We acknowledge that the people of this land maintain knowledge and educate others through

an oral tradition practised for generations.
